In the year 2200, advanced artificial intelligence led
to the widespread production of robots, integrating them
into daily life. Erdem, a bright young man living in this
“Age of Robots,” was keen on learning and researching
to develop himself. He delved deeply into robot technology, aspiring to build his own robot and compete in
tournaments. To further his robotic education, Erdem
attended the Hirotoma Robot Academy in Japan, where
he made international friends. Alongside his friends, they
found themselves entangled in a conflict with an international criminal organization while developing their robots.
Thus, their grand adventure commenced. Can Erdem
and his friends overcome the criminal organization? Will
they protect their robots from harm? And can they navigate through the elimination stages to reach The Robot
Olympics?
