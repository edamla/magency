The tale depicts a discerning bear’s quest for friendship.
The bear evaluates others based on size, character, and
strength, considering itself noble while viewing others as
inferior. Eventually, it forms a friendship based on power and
similar traits, but later learns the value of effort, loyalty, and
solidarity. The story highlights how appearances can be deceptive in friendships and emphasizes the importance of self
awareness.
